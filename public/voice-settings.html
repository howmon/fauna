Voice settings

All values persist to ~/.config/fauna/voice-settings.json. Changes apply immediately.

Wake word

When Listen in background is on, Fauna only acts on utterances that include one of these phrases.

After Fauna answers you, anything you say within this window counts as a follow-up.

Speech (TTS)

Uses your operating system's built-in text-to-speech.

macOS: words/min (default ≈175). Linux: WPM. Windows: -10..10.

Dictation

Press the shortcut to start recording, press again to stop. Transcript is copied to the clipboard.

Electron accelerator syntax — e.g. Cmd+Shift+D, Ctrl+Alt+Space.

Device labels appear after you've granted mic permission once.

Whisper speech-to-text model

Larger models are more accurate but slower and use more disk. Models download from Hugging Face on first use.

Privacy & redaction

Applied before anything is written to facts.json or playbooks.json. Cloud keys, JWTs, and tokens are always redacted.

Tool budget
